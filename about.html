Our Story In Stone Finishing Industry


At Renaissance Stones, we specialize in transforming spaces with high-quality granite and marble. Based in Addis Ababa, we offer a diverse range of stone products tailored for both residential and commercial needs.





Our Mission

Our mission is to deliver exceptional stone solutions that enhance residential and commercial spaces' aesthetic appeal and functionality. We believe in the power of natural materials to create timeless designs that stand the test of time.

Why Choose Us For Interior Work

We import premium quality granite and marble in a diverse range of colors that you won’t find locally. This unique selection offers our clients exceptional options that stand out, making Renaissance Stones the go-to choice for luxury stone products.
